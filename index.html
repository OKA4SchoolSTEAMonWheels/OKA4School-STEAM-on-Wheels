About

A portable model rooted in dignity, creativity, and access.

OKA4School STEAM on Wheels exists to reach learners where they are, especially in schools and communities with limited resources. Rather than waiting for young people to find opportunity, the program carries practical experiences to them through mobile circles that blend wellbeing, hands-on making, and future-focused learning.

As a CBO, the organization can begin locally, build trust at community level, and document a strong pilot model before expanding to wider partnerships and fundraising.

Pilot Route 1

A small, mobile pilot designed for proof of concept.

The first route can focus on one school or two nearby communities, using a portable kit and a short cycle of sessions to test what works best before scaling.

  • 8–10 circle sessions over one school term.
  • 30–40 learners reached through one pilot site.
  • Baking, reflection, and simple STEAM mini-activities in each cycle.
  • Light monitoring using attendance, reflections, and facilitator notes.
